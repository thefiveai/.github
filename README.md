Five AI is at the forefront of developing cutting-edge artificial intelligence technologies for autonomous systems. Our research focuses on computer vision, deep learning, and robust AI systems that can operate safely and reliably in real-world environments.

We specialize in:

- **Advanced Computer Vision**: State-of-the-art object detection and recognition systems with superior accuracy and reliability
- **Model Calibration**: Industry-leading techniques for ensuring AI models provide trustworthy confidence estimates
- **Test-Time Adaptation**: Novel approaches for AI systems that can adapt to changing conditions without retraining
- **Safety-Critical AI**: Research and development of AI systems designed for deployment in safety-critical applications
- **Ensemble Methods**: Innovative mixture of experts approaches that combine multiple AI models for enhanced performance
